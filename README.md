# Hongjian Wang

PhD student in Electrical Engineering and Information Technology at ETH Zurich, and member of TOMCAT at the Paul Scherrer Institute.

I work around computational imaging, inverse problems, tomographic imaging, and physics-informed reconstruction. My current interests include:

- Computational imaging systems
- X-ray tomography and synchrotron imaging
- Inverse problems and learned priors
- Neural and diffusion-based reconstruction
- Scientific visualization and reproducible research tools
