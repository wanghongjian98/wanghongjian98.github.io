Hongjian Wang

PhD Student, ETH Zurich / Paul Scherrer Institute

I am a PhD student in Electrical Engineering and Information Technology at ETH Zurich, and a member of TOMCAT at the Paul Scherrer Institute. I started my PhD in August 2023, advised by Prof. Dr. Marco Stampanoni, Dr. Goran Lovric at the Swiss Light Source, and Dr. Benjamin Bejar Haro at the Swiss Data Science Center.

I work on computational imaging methods for X-ray tomography and dynamic imaging. My research combines sensor design, physical forward models, reconstruction algorithms, and learned priors to recover interpretable images from sparse, indirect, and time-resolved measurements.
