Hongjian Wang 王宏剑

I am a PhD student in Electrical Engineering and Information Technology at ETH Zürich, and a member of TOMCAT at the Paul Scherrer Institute.

My research focuses on computational imaging: combining measurement systems, physical models, reconstruction algorithms, and data-driven priors to turn indirect signals into interpretable images. I started my PhD in August 2023, advised by Prof. Dr. Marco Stampanoni, Dr. Goran Lovric at Swiss Light Source, and Dr. Benjamín Béjar Haro at the Swiss Data Science Center.
